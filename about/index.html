About

Hello, my name is William Pereira and I’m a Software Developer. The intent of this blog is to post some of my personal experiences and share my knowledge with people from around the world.

Talking a little about me I like to think that I’m in a continuous search for my purpose in the world, either it would be working as a dev and writing chronicles about day-to-day knowledge or talking about my hobbies. I hope those projects are of interest for you and I’ll try to tag them in the best possible way for a quick understanding of the topic.

You can contact me by email with willpereirabr@gmail.com or can see more of my experience on my linkedin: https://www.linkedin.com/in/willpereira/.

Please enjoy your reading!
